## Debrief email (after Session 4)

**Subject:** Study debrief — thank you for participating

Hi [NAME],

Thank you for completing all four sessions of our study.

**What this study was about:**

We were investigating whether people tend to rely on AI assistance regardless of how difficult a task is, or whether they use it more selectively — only when a task is genuinely hard. We were also interested in whether heavy AI reliance in Sessions 1–3 affects independent reasoning ability in Session 4, where no AI was available.

Your participation will help us better understand how AI tools affect cognitive engagement in academic and professional settings.

**Your data:**
- All responses are stored anonymously using your Participant ID only
- No personal information is linked to your responses
- Data will be used solely for academic research and may be published in anonymised form

If you have any questions about the study or would like to see the results once published, please reply to this email.

Thank you again for your time and contribution.
